IX. National Tourism Conference

Topic: Sustainable Tourism

May 3 - May 6, 2023 (Wednesday - Saturday) from 10:00 to 20:00,

Alanya, Kaila Beach Hotel

REGISTER NOW

GUEST SPEAKERS

Brian Mullis

Overtourism and undertourism

Destination Management Specialist for more than 25 years

He founded and led Sustainable Travel International, a global leader in the responsible tourism movement. Throughout his career, he has assisted hundreds of businesses and destinations worldwide in implementing innovative sustainable tourism initiatives that generate positive socio-economic and conservation outcomes in communities and protected areas.

Jonathon Day

Tourism Changemakers - the power of social entrepreneurs

Hospitality and Tourism Management Prof. at Purdue University

He is committed to ensuring tourism is a force for good in the world. He has worked on sustainable tourism and responsible travel projects in destinations from Australia to the Caribbean, Colombia to Nepal, as well as the United States.

Willy Legrand

The Art and Science of Sustainably Managing Hotels

Hospitality Management Prof. at the IUBH - Bonn, Germany

Prof. Willy will review the best practices in how best to manage hotels which are not hooked to any system, with a detailed description of the challenges and opportunities and an emphasis on recommendations to those investing in and operating those properties.

Kelly Bricker

The Destination and Industry Global Sustainable Tourism Criteria and Sustainable Tourism

Prof. at College of Health - USA

Her research interests center on sustainable tourism and impacts on communities and the environment. I have been funded to develop sustainable tourism guidelines for the tourism industry in North America; explore sustainable tourism certification programs in the USA
